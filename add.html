ADDITION

Addition is the 5th operator in the BODMAS rule.

Any Additions are done after anything in brackets, orders, divisions and multiplications are done first.

Addition has the mathematical operation of '+'.

Remember the order in which you work through a calculation is according to ORDER OF IMPORTANCE.

Below are some examples of BODMAS questions using addition to give you an idea:

  • 6 + 43 = 6 + 64 = 70
  • (9 + 35) ÷ 11 + 8 = 44 ÷ 11 + 8 = 4 + 8 = 12
  • 3 + 4 + 5 x (6 x 7) 1 = 3 + 4 + 5 x 42 = 3 + 4 + 210 = 7 + 120 = 217
